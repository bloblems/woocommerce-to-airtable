# Changelog

## [1.1] - 2023-07-12

### Added
- Resend to Airtable Action: A new action button on the admin order page that allows the order data to be manually resent to Airtable.
- Enhanced Data Formatting: SKU instead of the product name, and the order quantity shows the SKU and quantity in brackets.
- Capitalization of Names: The customer's first and last names are now being capitalized before being sent to Airtable.
